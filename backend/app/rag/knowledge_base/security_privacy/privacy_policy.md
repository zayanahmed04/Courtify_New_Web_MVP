## Your Rights

### Access Your Data

You can request to see all data Courtify holds about you:
1. Contact Courtify support
2. Request data access
3. Provide identity verification
4. Receive your data (typically within 30 days)
5. Data provided in readable format

### Correct Your Data

If information is inaccurate:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to account settings
3. Update incorrect information
4. Save changes
5. Information corrected immediately

**Examples**: Wrong email, incorrect phone, outdated profile info

### Delete Your Data

You can request your data be deleted:
1. Contact Courtify support
2. Request account/data deletion
3. Verify your identity
4. Some data may be retained for legal reasons
5. Confirmation provided when complete

**Limitations**: Tax records, legal documents may be retained

### Download Your Data

Request a copy of all your data:
1. Request data export
2. Receive download link
3. Data in portable format (CSV, PDF, etc.)
4. Complete copy of your information
5. Timeline: typically 30 days

### Opt-Out of Communications

You can control what communications you receive:
1. Go to notification preferences
2. Choose which types of emails to receive
3. Unsubscribe from specific notification types
4. Some essential emails can't be disabled (booking updates)
5. Changes take effect immediately

**Note**: We must send essential booking/payment notifications

### Restrict Processing

You can limit how your data is used:
1. Some processing is necessary for services
2. Other processing is optional
3. You can restrict non-essential processing
4. This may limit platform functionality
5. Contact support to discuss options

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Information

**Active Account**:
- Account data: Kept while account active
- Booking history: Indefinitely for reference
- Communications: Kept for dispute resolution

**Inactive/Deleted Account**:
- Personal data: Removed after deletion request
- Tax/legal records: Kept per legal requirements (typically 7 years)
- Anonymized data: May be retained for analytics
- Backups: Purged according to backup schedule

**Specific Data**:
- Passwords: Not stored, only hash
- Card details: Not stored (Stripe's responsibility)
- Messages: Kept indefinitely unless deleted
- Support tickets: Kept for 2+ years for reference

### Deletion Process

When you request deletion:
1. Your account is marked for deletion
2. Personal data removed from live systems
3. Access to your account immediately revoked
4. Data purged from backups after retention period
5. Confirmation provided when complete
