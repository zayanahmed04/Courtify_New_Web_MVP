# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Quick Answers to Common Questions

This section provides quick answers to frequently asked questions from all user types. For detailed information, refer to the specific guides mentioned or contact support.

---

## Account & Registration

### Q: How do I create an account?
**A**: 
1. Go to Courtify website
2. Click "Register" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a secure password
5. Select your user type (Player or Court Owner)
6. Complete your profile
7. Verify your email
8. Account ready to use

### Q: What information is required to register?
**A**: Minimum required:
- Email address (verified)
- Password
- User type (player or owner)
- Basic profile info (optional but recommended)

Optional: Phone number, gender, profile picture

### Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
**A**: 
1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click link (valid for 24 hours)
6. Create new password
7. Login with new password

If you don't receive email, check spam folder or contact support.

### Q: Can I change my user type later?
**A**: No. User type (Player or Owner) is set at registration. If you need both roles, create separate accounts with different email addresses.

### Q: Can I delete my account?
**A**: Yes. Go to Account Settings > Delete Account. Your data will be removed (subject to legal retention requirements). Active bookings will be cancelled.

### Q: Is my password secure?
**A**: Yes. Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard security. Courtify never stores actual passwords, only encrypted versions. Never share your password with anyone.

## Booking Questions

### Q: How do I book a court?
**A**:
1. Search for courts (by location, date, time)
2. Click on court you like
3. Select date and time slot
4. Click "Book"
5. Review booking details
6. Submit booking request
7. Wait for owner confirmation (usually quick)
8. Upon approval, proceed to payment

### Q: What does "Pending" status mean?
**A**: Your booking request is waiting for the court owner to accept it. Owners typically respond within a few hours. If not confirmed within 24 hours, contact the owner through the platform.

### Q: How much time do I have to pay after booking is approved?
**A**: Payment is required immediately upon approval to confirm the booking. If you don't pay within the same session, the booking request may expire.

### Q: Can I book the same slot multiple times?
**A**: No. Each slot can only be booked once. Once a slot is booked and confirmed by another player, it won't be available. Choose an alternative time if needed.

### Q: What if I need to cancel my booking?
**A**:
1. Go to your bookings
2. Find the booking to cancel
3. Click "Cancel Booking"
4. Follow cancellation process
5. Refund eligibility depends on cancellation timing

Check refund policy for details on refund amounts.

### Q: What happens if a court owner rejects my booking?
**A**: Rejection means the owner couldn't accommodate your request. You won't be charged. You can send a message asking why, or try booking another time or court.

### Q: How far in advance can I book?
**A**: This varies by court. Some courts allow bookings weeks in advance, others only a few days. Check the individual court listing for their booking window.

### Q: Can I modify a booking after it's confirmed?
**A**: Direct modification isn't available. Options:
- Cancel and rebook a different time (subject to refund policy)
- Contact owner directly to negotiate changes
- Contact support for special circumstances

## Payment & Refunds

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A**: Courtify accepts:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, etc.)
- Debit cards
- Other card types supported by Stripe
- All processed securely through Stripe

### Q: Is it safe to enter my card information?
**A**: Yes. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, an industry-leading secure payment processor. Courtify never sees your full card details. Your information is encrypted and PCI-compliant.

### Q: My card was declined. What do I do?
**A**: Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Wrong details entered
- Bank blocked transaction
- Try again or use different card
- Contact your bank if issue persists
- Contact support for assistance

### Q: When will I be charged?
**A**: You're charged immediately after clicking "Complete Payment" following a booking approval. You'll receive a confirmation email with receipt.

### Q: Can I get a refund?
**A**: Yes, depending on when you cancel:
- Cancel well in advance: Full refund
- Cancel closer to booking time: Partial or no refund
- See Refunds guide for specific timeline

Admin may override for special circumstances.

### Q: How long does a refund take?
**A**: Typically 5-10 business days from cancellation approval. Time depends on your bank. Check your bank if longer than 10 days.

### Q: Do I get a receipt?
**A**: Yes. Receipt is emailed immediately and available anytime in your booking details. Download, print, or screenshot as needed.

### Q: Are there any additional fees?
**A**: Prices shown are total. No hidden charges. What you see is what you pay (plus taxes if applicable by location).

## Searching & Finding Courts

### Q: How do I search for courts?
**A**:
1. Go to "Find Courts" or main page
2. Enter location or court name
3. Select date and time
4. Set other filters (price range, features, rating)
5. Click search
6. Browse results
7. Click court for details
8. Make booking

### Q: Can I sort results by price?
**A**: Yes. Most search results allow sorting by:
- Price (low to high or high to low)
- Rating (highest first)
- Distance (closest first)
- Popularity (most booked)

### Q: What does the star rating mean?
**A**: Star ratings are based on reviews from players who booked at that court:
- 5 stars: Excellent
- 4 stars: Very good
- 3 stars: Good
- 2 stars: Fair
- 1 star: Poor

Average rating shown on all court listings.

### Q: Can I see reviews before booking?
**A**: Yes. Click any court to see detailed reviews from other players. Reviews include written feedback and ratings. This helps you decide if it's right for you.

### Q: What if I can't find a court I'm looking for?
**A**: Try:
- Search nearby locations
- Adjust date/time
- Expand your price range
- Remove filters
- Contact support for recommendations

If court doesn't exist, suggest to owner to register it!

### Q: Can I save my favorite courts?
**A**: Yes. Click heart icon on court listing to add to favorites. Access favorites anytime from your dashboard for quick rebooking.

## Reviewing & Ratings

### Q: How do I leave a review?
**A**: 
1. After your booking is completed
2. You'll see "Leave Review" option
3. Click to open review form
4. Rate 1-5 stars
5. Write optional feedback
6. Submit
7. Review posted (after moderation)

### Q: Can I change my review?
**A**: You can usually delete and resubmit. Contact support if you need help editing an existing review.

### Q: Can I see reviews before booking?
**A**: Yes. Reviews are visible on the court listing page. Read what other players say before deciding.

### Q: What if I received poor service? Should I leave a bad review?
**A**: Yes, be honest. But first:
1. Try resolving with the owner
2. Contact support if issue unresolved
3. Leave honest review describing experience
4. Be constructive, not personal
5. Describe specific issues

### Q: Can a review be removed?
**A**: Courtify reviews moderation team. Reviews may be removed if they:
- Violate community guidelines
- Contain false information
- Are spam or promotional
- Contain personal attacks

Genuine negative reviews are kept.

## Court Owner Questions

### Q: How do I register my court?
**A**:
1. Create account as "Court Owner"
2. Go to "Register Court"
3. Enter court details (name, location, description)
4. Set operating hours
5. Set pricing for time slots
6. Upload court photos
7. Add amenities/features
8. Submit for admin approval
9. Wait for approval (usually 1-3 days)
10. Court goes live

### Q: What happens after I register my court?
**A**: Court is reviewed by Courtify admins for:
- Accuracy of information
- Valid location
- Appropriate pricing
- Community guidelines compliance

You'll be notified if approved or if changes needed.

### Q: How do I manage my court's availability?
**A**: In your owner dashboard:
1. Go to "My Courts"
2. Select court
3. Click "Edit Availability"
4. Set operating hours
5. Mark special closures
6. Set pricing for different times
7. Save changes
8. Changes take effect immediately

### Q: Can I change pricing?
**A**: Yes. Change pricing anytime in your court settings. Changes apply to future bookings only. Existing confirmed bookings keep their original price.

### Q: How do I handle booking requests?
**A**: When you receive a request:
1. View request details
2. Check availability
3. Accept or reject
4. Player proceeds to payment (if accepted)
5. Booking confirmed once paid
6. Track in your dashboard

### Q: What if I need to cancel a confirmed booking?
**A**: You can cancel, but:
- Player is entitled to full refund
- You may be penalized in ratings
- Only cancel for valid reasons (emergency, facility issue, etc.)
- Communicate reason to player

### Q: How do I get paid?
**A**: Payment flow:
1. Player books and pays
2. Payment processed by Stripe
3. Funds held pending completion
4. After booking completes, funds released
5. Funds transferred to your account (varies by bank, typically 2-5 days)

Note: You don't have bookings to "accept" payment for - payment happens automatically when player completes booking.

### Q: Can I see my earnings?
**A**: Yes. In your owner dashboard:
- View total earnings
- See breakdown by court
- Track payments received
- See pending payouts
- Download financial reports

### Q: What if a player leaves a bad review?
**A**: 
1. Read the review carefully
2. If issue is genuine, address concerns
3. You can respond to reviews (contact support)
4. Improve your service to increase ratings
5. Collect positive reviews through good service

Focus on quality to maintain high ratings.

### Q: Can I see how many bookings I'm getting?
**A**: Yes. Your dashboard shows:
- Total bookings this month
- Bookings by court
- Average occupancy rate
- Trending vs. last month
- Comparison with other courts

Use this data to optimize pricing/hours.
