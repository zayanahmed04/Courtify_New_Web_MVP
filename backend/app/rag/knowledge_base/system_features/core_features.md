# Core Features of Courtify

## Platform Features Overview

Courtify is a comprehensive online court booking platform designed to connect players with available courts and enable court owners to manage their facilities efficiently. This document outlines all major features available on the platform and how they work together to create a seamless experience.

## User Types and Access

### Three Main User Types

**Players/Regular Users**:
- Browse and search courts
- Book available time slots
- Track bookings
- Make payments
- Leave reviews and ratings
- Connect with other players
- Manage favorites

**Court Owners**:
- Register and manage courts
- Set pricing and availability
- Accept/reject booking requests
- Communicate with players
- Track earnings
- View platform statistics
- Update court information

**Administrators**:
- Oversee entire platform
- Approve/reject new courts
- Manage user accounts
- View platform statistics
- Monitor transactions
- Handle disputes
- Maintain platform integrity

## Booking System

### Making a Booking

**Booking Process**:
1. Select court and date/time
2. Review availability
3. Confirm booking details
4. Submit booking request
5. Wait for owner confirmation
6. Receive confirmation (or rejection)
7. Proceed to payment if approved
8. Confirm receipt
9. Show up for your booking

**Booking Status Tracking**:
- Pending: Awaiting court owner response
- Approved: Owner confirmed, ready for payment
- Confirmed: Payment received, you're all set
- Completed: Booking time has passed
- Rejected: Owner declined request
- Cancelled: You or owner cancelled

**Booking Details**:
- Court name and location
- Date and time
- Duration
- Price
- Owner contact information
- Special instructions/notes
- Payment status
- Cancel deadline

### Managing Bookings (Owner Feature)

**Request Management**:
- View incoming booking requests
- Accept bookings (player proceeds to payment)
- Reject bookings (with optional message)
- See booking details
- Track request timeline
- Filter requests by status

**Cancellation Handling**:
- Review cancellation requests
- Accept/reject player cancellations
- Process refunds
- Communicate with players
- Track cancellation patterns
- Handle disputes

**Booking Calendar**:
- Visual calendar view
- See all bookings
- View availability
- Mark special days/closures
- Quick status updates
- Export booking data

## Payment System

### Payment Processing

**Payment Methods**:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
- Debit cards
- Secure online payment
- Powered by Stripe (industry standard)

**Payment Flow**:
1. Booking approved by owner
2. Player proceeds to payment
3. Enter card details securely
4. Payment processed
5. Confirmation received
6. Receipt emailed
7. Booking confirmed

**Payment Information**:
- Only card number needed (stored securely by Stripe)
- Courtify never sees full card details
- PCI-compliant security
- Encrypted transmission
- Multiple payment attempts allowed

### Refunds & Financial Management

**Refund Policy**:
- Refund eligibility depends on cancellation time
- Full refund if cancelled per policy
- Partial/no refund if cancelled late
- Admin can override for disputes
- Process typically takes 5-10 business days
- Automatic refund processing

**Payment History**:
- View all past payments
- Download receipts anytime
- Track refund status
- See payment dates and amounts
- Detailed transaction records
- Year-end summary for taxes

**Payment Issues**:
- Card declined? Try again or use different card
- Payment stuck? Contact support
- Unexpected charge? Contact support immediately
- Refund questions? Check policy or contact support

## Reviews & Ratings System

### Leaving Reviews

**Review Components**:
- Star rating (1-5 stars)
- Written review (optional)
- Feedback on specific aspects
- Anonymous option (optional)
- Photos can be attached

**Review Process**:
1. Complete your booking
2. Booking marked as completed
3. Invite to review appears
4. Write your feedback
5. Rate your experience
6. Submit review
7. Appears on court listing (after moderation)

**Review Guidelines**:
- Honest and constructive feedback
- No spam or promotional content
- No personal attacks
- Focus on facility, not individuals
- Relevant to court experience
- Community guidelines enforced

### Court Ratings

**Rating System**:
- Average star rating calculated
- Multiple reviews increase credibility
- Rating influences court visibility
- Low ratings flagged for review
- Very low ratings may be investigated
- Admin can remove inappropriate reviews

**Rating Impact**:
- Higher ratings appear in search first
- Rating affects court popularity
- Players view ratings before booking
- Owners notified of low ratings
- Improvement path discussed
